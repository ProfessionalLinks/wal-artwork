Understand Your Room’s Color Palette

Before choosing wall artwork, it’s essential to understand the color palette of the room where it will be displayed. The artwork you select should either contrast with or complement these colors to create a balanced look.

For example, in a room with neutral tones, vibrant artwork can add a pop of color, energizing the space. Conversely, if the room features bold colors, you may want to select more subtle, monochromatic artwork to keep the room feeling cohesive.

At DesiWall, their diverse collection offers a wide range of pieces that suit various color schemes, whether you want to make a bold statement or create a calm, subdued atmosphere.

Consider the Size and Scale of the Artwork

The size of the wall artwork is crucial in maintaining balance and harmony in your room. A large piece of art can dominate a wall and become a striking focal point, while smaller pieces are better suited for tight spaces or when grouped to form a gallery wall.

When choosing the right size, ensure the piece is proportional to the furniture in the room. For example, over a sofa or large console table, a large or medium-sized artwork works best. If you’re creating a gallery wall, mix different sizes to add variety and interest.

DesiWall offers a range of sizes to suit all spaces, from small accent pieces to large statement artworks. Plus, you can visit them by Google map to view their full collection in person.

Match the Art Style to Your Decor

The style of your wall artwork should align with the overall theme and mood of your room. If your decor is minimalist, choose simple, clean designs, such as line art or abstract pieces. For a more traditional space, landscapes or classic portraits may be more fitting.

Consider the aesthetic of the room—modern, bohemian, industrial, vintage, etc.—and select art that reflects the style. You can also mix styles if done thoughtfully; for example, pairing contemporary artwork with vintage furniture can create a unique and personalized vibe.

DesiWall offers various art styles, ranging from modern abstracts to traditional paintings, allowing you to find the perfect match for your home’s design.

Keep Your Personal Style in Mind

When selecting wall artwork, it’s essential to keep your own tastes and interests in mind. Art is a form of self-expression, and it should evoke emotions or memories that are meaningful to you. Whether you prefer vibrant and bold art or soft, subtle pieces, make sure the art resonates with your style.

If you’re unsure about what you like, take time to browse through different genres—nature, portraiture, geometric patterns, or minimalism. A piece of art that reflects your personality can significantly improve the overall vibe of your living space.

Experiment with Placement

Where you hang the wall artwork can have a significant impact on its effect. For larger pieces, consider placing them above a focal point, such as a couch or fireplace. Smaller pieces are best arranged in groupings on a feature wall or spread evenly across a room.

Don’t hesitate to experiment with placement by laying the pieces on the floor first to find the arrangement that feels right. You can also use painter’s tape to mark the area where the artwork will go, helping you visualize it before committing to hanging.

Embrace Lighting to Highlight Your Art

The right lighting can dramatically enhance the appearance of your wall artwork. Consider using wall-mounted lights or picture lights that direct light onto the artwork to create a gallery effect. You can also use ambient lighting, like floor lamps or pendant lights, to make the artwork stand out even more.

The use of lighting not only highlights the colors and details in the artwork but also helps set the mood in the room.
